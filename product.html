The Louvered Rover will be fully exposed to the elements for many years. Fortunately, we've designed many features to provide a low-maintenance experience. For example, fully rotate the louvers for cleaning mode; this allows you to simply clean the top of the louvers with a water hose. Another favorite feature is the built-in gutter screen track. Simply acquire gutter screens from the local hardware store and insert the screen into the track to prevent debris from clogging the gutters. We recommend annually cleaning the gutter screens.

The Louvered Roof is not only designed for protection from sun. First, we engineered a tectonic arch into our louvered roof to provide two layers of extruded aluminum to offer maximum heat resistance. The top section absorbs the heat without transmitting it to the bottom section. Second we designed our motorized louvered roof to provide ventilation. If the air under the covering is too hot, fully rotate the louvers. This feature completely shades and ventilates the are at the same time.
